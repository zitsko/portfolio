Gyrenia Guest House

Rooms to Rent website

A website made with WordPress for Cyrenia Guest House, a hotel located in Monemvasia. In the Home page, the user can quickly see general information about the hotel, photos and info about the provided rooms, a sample of the services to the customers and go to the contact us page. By navigating to the menu in the header, the user can see more details and photos for the hotel rooms, the full list of the services, check for activities to do in the area and obtain contact information like location, phone, mail and Facebook.The site is bilingual since there is the ability to switch between Greek and English language by pressing the corresponding flags in the header. Also at the footer of the site a Facebook widget links to the Facebook page of the hotel for the user to follow the page or watch the posts in the feed.

Cyrenia.gr was built with WordPress and Ocean WP theme to control the header and the footer, Elementor WordPress builder for the body of the website and other plugins for cookies compliance and security with SSL.
