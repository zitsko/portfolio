Home Sweet Home

Real Estate Management

Home Sweet Home is Web page where admins can share and manage their houses from the admin page where they can fully describe the home and set a price for it, and then the users can explore these houses rate them and review them.

Home Sweet Home was built with React, Node.js, MongoDB, Bootstrap, and Express.js
