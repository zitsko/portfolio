LinkShare App

A personal digital card .

The primary objective of LinkShare application is to build a section for people to be able to gather all their social media links and personal details and share them all together to other people,like a personal digital card. LinkShare can be used from a developer or any other person that wants to share his online presence with ease and boost his networking.

The technologies used were MongoDb with Mongoose,Express.js,React.js and Node.js(MERN Stack),Cloudinary cloud for uploading and storing images and FontAwesome library for icons. Also for styling I created my own custom library in CSS and applied it.
