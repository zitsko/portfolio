Civil Engineers Office

This is a website made for a Civil Engineer's Office where the user can obtain general information about the office, the projects made by the office, the people that work there, contact info and location. I build this site with collaboration with a friend of mine for the founder of Mnb-Engineers to help him showcase his work on the internet. For mnb.gr we used WordPress combined with Ocean WP for the theme, Elementor for building the larger part of the website and Unlimited Elements for Elementor to add interactive widgets like Flip Box for the business cards. The site is secure for browsing since it is encrypted following the SSL protocol and has the necessary cookies for compliance with GDPR / CCPA.
