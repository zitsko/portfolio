Workout Tracker

Fitness Management

The Workout Tracker App is a web application where fitness enthusiasts can manage and track their workout progress. In the platform the user create and customize a workout routine and take inspiration by searching a workout library filled with exercises.

The technologies used were MongoDb with Mongoose,Express.js,React.js and Node.js(MERN Stack), and integration with the Ninza API.
